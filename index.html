Our Story

Founded in 1986 by Christine Bell, PLOP-JON has provided dependable portable sanitation to thousands of events and job sites across Florida. We started with just a few units and a single truck—and thanks to word-of-mouth and loyal clients, we've grown into one of the Treasure Coast’s most trusted names.
